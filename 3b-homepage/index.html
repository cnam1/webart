About

My name is Choonghyun Charlie Nam.
I am an interdisciplinary artist currently studying at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago. I studied architecture and sculpture, and I am currently focusing mostly on photography, film, and art/technology. My creative practice is an exploration of the intrinsic connection between these disciplines.

This leads to my fascination with architecture. Architecture serves as both muse and medium in my artistic expression. The lines, shapes, and spaces converge to create stunning visual compositions. I am interested in particularly in photography, where I strive to capture the essence of architectural form.
